PRIVACY POLICY

i‑Terek – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how i‑Terek (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses and protects information when you use our mobile application published on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Location Information

If you grant the app permission to access your device’s location, we use your approximate location (latitude and longitude) to show weather and related indicators for your area. If you do not grant permission, you can manually choose a city, or the app may use a default city (Astana).

We do not track your movement history and do not store your routes.

1.2. Technical and Usage Information

We may automatically collect technical information such as device model and OS version, app version, language and country settings, and basic usage information (for example, crashes and technical errors) to ensure stable operation.

1.3. Weather and External Services Data

To show forecasts and related indicators, the app sends requests to third‑party weather services (for example, OpenWeather, Tomorrow.io or other publicly available APIs), transferring only the minimum data needed to get weather information (coordinates or city name).

1.4. Local Storage on Your Device

The app uses local storage to save your selected city, last known coordinates, theme, settings and last downloaded weather data. This data is stored only on your device and is not automatically sent to our own servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • to provide the main functions of the app (weather and indicators for your location);
  • to adapt the interface and theme;
  • to analyze and improve the stability and quality of the app;
  • to detect and fix bugs and technical problems;
  • to respond to your requests if you contact us via email.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information only with:

  • weather providers and external APIs (to obtain forecasts);
  • service providers that help us with analytics and error logging;
  • authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis (for EEA Users)

For users in the European Economic Area we process personal data based on: your consent (for location access), our legitimate interests (to ensure security and improve the app), and performance of a contract (to provide the app’s core functionality).

5. International Transfers

Weather providers and technical services used by the app may process data on servers outside your country of residence. We take reasonable steps to ensure that such services handle data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your local law, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion or restriction of processing of your personal data, as well as the right to withdraw your consent for location access at any time via device settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality or as required by applicable laws. Local data stored on your device remains there until you delete the app or clear its data.

8. Third‑Party Services

Our app may use third‑party services (such as weather APIs or analytics tools). Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not store or access passwords from third‑party accounts.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will publish the updated version and update the “Last updated” date at the top. Significant changes may be additionally communicated through a notice in the app.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the email address listed in the developer contact section on Google Play for i‑Terek.

This document is provided for informational purposes and does not create any contractual relationship between you and i‑Terek beyond what is described in the app and applicable store terms.
